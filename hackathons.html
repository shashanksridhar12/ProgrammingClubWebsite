Hackathons

Amazing coding events intented for anyone interested in coding

What is a Hackathon?

  • A Hackathon is a 12-24 hour coding event where both experienced and inexperienced programmers can team up to build cool hacks
  • The cost of the hackathons are free! That means: Free Food!! Free T-shirts!! Free Swag!
  • For every hackathon, NO coding experience is needed. There will be numerous workshops at the hackathon teaching different levels of coding, and they are meant for beginners and advanced coders to attend
  • All Hackathons are sponsored by many different companies like github, google, etc so the prizes range from quadcopters to $200 in cash to Virtual Reality headseats.
