About Us

The Programming Club teaches its members how to code in numerous programming languages. The club's goal is to get members interested in computer science as well as help the students build projects of their own.

Furthermore, the Programming Club prepares its members for coding competitions such as USACO (USA Computing Olympiad), and also for hackathons, which are fun, interactive events where high school students code an app with their group for 24 hours.
